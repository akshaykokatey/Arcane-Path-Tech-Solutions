Internship Cancellation and Refund Policies:

The cancellation and refund policy for an internship will vary depending on the internship provider. Here are some common policies:

  • No refund after payment: Some internship providers do not offer refunds at all, once you have paid for the internship. This is often the case for internships that are highly competitive or that have limited spots.
  • Refund within a certain period of time: Other internship providers may offer a refund within a certain period of time after you have paid, such as 14 days or 30 days. This is typically the case for internships that are not as competitive or that have more spots available.
  • Refund for extenuating circumstances: Some internship providers may offer a refund if you have to cancel due to extenuating circumstances, such as a medical emergency or a family death. You will need to provide documentation to support your request for a refund.
  • Prorated refund: If you cancel an internship after the start date, you may only be eligible for a prorated refund. This means that you will only be refunded for the portion of the internship that you did not participate in.
